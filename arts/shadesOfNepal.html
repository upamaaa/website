SHADES OF NEPAL

2018, 12” x 16”, 24 hours
Soft pastel

I have drawn this sketch as an honor to the Nepalese way of living. One can see a Nepali woman adorned in the elegant gunyo cholo as she radiates warmth and cultural richness. Her traditional attire symbolizes heritage, resilience, and the enduring spirit of Nepal's cultural tapestry. The nose ring, a central adornment, embodies tradition and signifies a connection to the land and its values.
