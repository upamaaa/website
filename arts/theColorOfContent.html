THE COLOR OF CONTENT

2018, 13” x 13”, 30 hours
Soft pastel

Here, the canvas is not just the picture but his face. Amidst the soft, blended background, the boy’s disheveled hair adds to the lively chaos. His penetrating gaze hints at a depth beyond the colorful façade. Maybe it’s his innocence, mischief, or the unbridled thrill of the moment. One can only wonder.
