SERENADE OF THE WOODS

2023, 22” x 30”, 105 hours
Acrylic

I drew this painting with a reference derived from a traditional landscape painter, Erik Koeppel. But after invoking a series of thoughts and incorporating color palette modifications, this painting has become my dreamscape. It has given me the opportunity to explore a differing medium from graphite and charcoal. Exploring the art of serenity, this artwork resides in the liminal space between a bustling existence of life and a celestial allure of escape.
