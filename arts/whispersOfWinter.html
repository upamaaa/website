WHISPERS OF WINTER

2023, 16” x 12”, 3 hours
Acrylic

In an interesting interplay of elements, I have tried to capture a fog-laden mountain shrouded in gentle mist and undisturbed snow. Amidst the tranquility, the foreground unfolds—an expanse of lush grasses, pine trees and a crystal-clear lake that mirrors the ethereal scene with its glistening surface. The juxtaposition of greenery against the snow-laden peaks invites one to find solace in the harmony of nature's contrasting yet harmonious facets. One can tell that landscapes like these invokes a calming sensation in my mind. Also, it's one of the quickest paintings I have drawn in a while.
